Segment 1 — Brand, Entity, SEO/GEO/Local/Ads/PPC Fulfillment, CRM & Automations
Phase 1

Brand + Entity

You cannot sell authority you do not possess. Before attempting to acquire clients or deploy fulfillment systems, the agency owner must become a verified, machine-readable entity in the semantic web. This phase gives participants immediate, tangible assets that build confidence before the more technically demanding phases ahead.
Session 1
Virtual

The Digital Identity Foundation

Establish the participant's digital entity architecture and operational baseline.

Core Deliverables

Website Architecture via Website Studio — Build or rebuild web properties with semantic HTML5 elements optimized for machine readability and AI extraction.
Schema.org Structured Data Deployment — Deploy comprehensive Organization and Person schemas with sameAs, founder, knowsAbout, and hasCredential properties to feed the Google Knowledge Graph.
Executive Automation Setup — Configure AI agents for email and scheduling via MCP servers, reclaiming 5-10 hours per week of administrative overhead.
Phase 2

SEO/GEO/PPC Fulfillment & Case Studies

With the brand entity established, participants now build the fulfillment engine that generates measurable results. This phase is the backbone of the entire mastermind: every case study generated here becomes sales ammunition in Phase 3, and every automated workflow becomes the delivery system for future client retainers.
Session 2
Virtual

Topical Relevance Engineering

Master the science of topical authority mapping and semantic content architecture.

Core Deliverables

Semantic Cluster Mapping — Identify complete semantic clusters using Content Genius and searchatlas_content MCP tools: parent topics, subtopics, entities, and semantic gaps.
Content Architecture Design — Produce comprehensive content architecture defining pillar pages, cluster content, internal linking topology, and publication sequencing.
Content Audit & Pruning — Run relevance scoring algorithms to identify pages diluting domain authority for pruning, merging, or rewriting.
GEO-Specific Content Structuring — Implement BLUF structures, citation clusters, FAQ blocks for AI extraction, and semantic HTML patterns maximizing LLM citation probability.
Session 3
Virtual

Sitewide Content Generation at Scale

Deploy industrial-scale, AI-driven content production pipelines that maintain quality and semantic integrity.

Core Deliverables

Automated Drafting Protocols — Generate semantically rich articles at industrial scale with automatic internal linking, FAQ sections, and strict semantic HTML hierarchies.
Universal CMS Deployment — Push engineered content directly to staging or live domains via 1-click publishing across multiple client sites.
Content Lifecycle Management — Configure automated decay detection and refresh workflows, transforming content into self-maintaining assets.
Case Study Documentation — Begin systematically capturing baseline metrics, actions, outcomes, traffic graphs, and revenue attribution.
Session 4
Virtual

Large-Scale Authority Building

Build unassailable domain authority through automated digital PR and strategic link acquisition.

Core Deliverables

Automated Link Acquisition — Utilize LinkLabs and outreach tools for automated HARO pitches, broken link campaigns, and partnership opportunities.
Digital PR Campaigns — Orchestrate multi-channel PR combining Authority Trophy assets with targeted outreach for high-authority editorial backlinks.
LLM Visibility Tracking — Track brand citations in LLM outputs: share of voice, sentiment, and citation frequency across models.
Case Study Completion — Compile first complete case studies with real before/after data to power Phase 3 sales.
Session 5
Virtual

Local SEO & Google Business Profile Dominance

Translate broad entity authority into hyper-local search dominance and capture high-intent leads in specific geographic markets.

Core Deliverables

Google Business Profile Optimization — Claim, verify, and aggressively optimize GBPs using the searchatlas_gbp tool and GBP Galactic features with automated review management and citation building.
Local Grid Tracking & Heatmaps — Deploy grid-based rank tracking to visualize local search dominance across geographic zones and identify coverage gaps.
AI-Powered Local Content — Generate hyper-relevant local posts, brand-aligned review responses, and geo-targeted landing pages at scale.
Multi-Location Management — Build scalable workflows for managing dozens of client GBP profiles simultaneously with automated posting, review monitoring, and citation consistency.
Session 6
Virtual

Paid Media & Landing Page Architecture

Master paid customer acquisition across Google and Facebook, coupled with high-converting landing page deployment.

Core Deliverables

Google Ads Automation — Automate campaign reporting, bid management, and performance analysis via OTTO PPC with keyword-level bidding and quality score optimization.
Facebook Ads Architecture — Build full-funnel campaigns: cold audience prospecting, warm retargeting, lookalike expansion with creative testing frameworks.
Landing Page Generation — Rapidly construct localized, conversion-optimized pages tailored to specific ad groups and geographic markets.
Multivariate Testing & CPA Optimization — Collaborative teardowns analyzing campaigns, identifying waste, and implementing systematic CPA reduction.
Paid + Organic Integration — Paid data feeds organic strategy; organic rankings reduce paid dependency. The flywheel effect is quantified and modeled.
Phase 3

CRM & Automations

With fulfillment systems producing results, the agency needs a central nervous system. CRM and automation infrastructure connects every touchpoint—leads, clients, campaigns, and communications—into a single orchestrated workflow. This phase eliminates manual handoffs and ensures no opportunity falls through the cracks.
Session 7
Virtual

CRM Architecture & Marketing Automation

Build the central automation backbone that connects lead generation, client management, and campaign execution into a single orchestrated system.

Core Deliverables

CRM Pipeline Architecture — Design and deploy multi-stage pipelines for leads, prospects, active clients, and upsell opportunities with automated stage transitions and task triggers.
Lead Nurture Sequences — Build automated email and messaging sequences triggered by prospect behavior: site visits, proposal views, form submissions, and engagement signals.
Client Lifecycle Automation — Automate the full client journey: onboarding workflows, milestone check-ins, renewal reminders, upsell triggers, and win-back campaigns for churned accounts.
Cross-Platform Integration — Connect CRM with ad platforms, analytics, Slack, email, and Search Atlas MCP tools for unified data flow and trigger-based automations across all channels.
Revenue Attribution & Forecasting — Build automated revenue tracking from first touch to closed deal, with pipeline forecasting models and LTV projections per acquisition channel.
Segment 2 — Prospecting, Sales, Reporting & Comms
Phase 4

Prospecting, Proposals & Sales

With a verified brand entity and documented case studies in hand, participants are credibly positioned to acquire clients. Outbound prospecting generates qualified leads, then sales mechanics and proposal engineering close them.
Session 8
Virtual

Programmatic Outbound & the "Clone Website" Strategy

Deploy the most potent cold outreach strategy in the modern agency playbook—delivering undeniable, personalized value at the top of the funnel.

Core Deliverables

Automated Technical Auditing — The Site Auditor crawls prospect websites, generating customized reports on technical failures, schema deficiencies, and Core Web Vitals.
"Clone Website" Mock-Ups — Build visually superior, structurally optimized mock-ups of prospect sites using Website Studio, making value propositions tangible.
Programmatic Outreach via MCP — Chain Search Atlas MCP with email integrations to identify prospects, run audits, generate mock-ups, and dispatch outreach automatically.
Case Study Integration — Every outreach email references Phase 2 case studies, creating a triple-layered value proposition: audit + mock-up + verified results.
Phase 5

Reporting & Client Communications

The highest cause of client churn in digital marketing is poor communication of value. With clients now on retainer, participants must demonstrate ongoing ROI with minimal manual effort. This phase transforms reporting from a time-consuming obligation into an automated competitive advantage.
Session 9
Virtual

Automated Dashboards & Client Communications

Build custom reporting dashboards and proactive communication systems that eliminate churn and compound perceived value without manual effort.

Core Deliverables

Custom HTML Dashboard Construction — Build self-contained, interactive reporting interfaces using Claude Code + Search Atlas MCP with D3.js visualizations.
Multi-Source Dashboard Architecture — Integrate organic/paid metrics, task tracking, and AI-generated executive summaries into unified dashboards.
Automated Slack Reporting — Secure webhooks deliver proactive, formatted updates on task completion and ranking movements into client Slack channels.
AI-Generated Narrative Reports — Weekly summaries translating metrics into business impact with revenue projections based on current conversion rates.
Churn Prevention Signals — Automated detection of declining engagement, missed calls, and ranking dips triggers proactive outreach before churn.
Client Onboarding Automation — Standardized sequences: welcome emails, access provisioning, baseline audits, first-30-day expectations documents.
Segment 3 — Perpetual Agents
Phase 6

Perpetual Agents — The 24/7 Agentic Agency

Everything built in Phases 1–5 still requires human initiation. Phase 6 removes that final constraint. The agency transitions from "automated with human triggers" to "autonomous with human oversight." This is the endgame: a marketing agency that operates continuously, self-corrects, and generates revenue while the owner sleeps.
Session 10
Virtual

The Autonomous Agency Blueprint

Deploy Perpetual Agents that monitor, optimize, and execute marketing operations 24/7—transforming the agency into a truly autonomous entity.

Core Deliverables

Perpetual Agent Architecture — Design continuous, self-healing loops: keyword monitors trigger content refreshes, technical SEO agents deploy patches, backlink monitors initiate recovery outreach.
Agent-to-Agent (A2A) Commerce Preparation — Structure client data and product catalogs for autonomous purchasing systems in the zero-click, automated marketplace.
Desktop & Mobile Command Interfaces — Configure streamlined approval interfaces for management by exception—only reviewing decisions exceeding confidence thresholds.
The 24/7 Agentic Agency Model — Map the complete autonomous flow: agents execute, dashboards report, clients receive comms, outbound prospects, proposals draft, owner approves.
3-to-5 Year Strategic Roadmap — Individualized plans covering scaling strategies, AI vs. human augmentation, acquisition exits, and positioning for the agentic commerce economy of 2027–2030.
